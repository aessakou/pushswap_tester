TESTER: make [option]
    Options:
        A : Test both the mandatory and bonus parts.
        M   : Test the mandatory part only.
        B   : Test the bonus part only.
        N   : Specify the number of tests. [e.g., make N=100] (default 50).
        R   : Specify the quantity of numbers you want to test. [e.g., make R=500].
        H   : Display this page. (e.g., make H)
